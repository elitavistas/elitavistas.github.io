Club Vistas


Swimming Pool:

Come take a refreshing plunge into the sparkling waters of the world-renowned Vistas Infinity Skypool on the 13th floor. The pool is open 24 hours with a lifeguard on duty. The water temperature is maintained according to the season and has been theme-lit by world-famous artists. Pool accessories are available for purchase.

Vistas Health Club:

Relax and revitalise your soul at our Health Club with advanced fitness equipment as well as steam and sauna facilities. Open daily from 0500 hours to 2300 hours. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, our gym provides personalised training programs overseen by experienced personal coaches.

Recreation Room:

Have fun with your friends or family at our Recreation Room to play billiards, air hockey, table tennis or TV games.

The Dhaba

The Dhaba, Elita Vistas, Chandigarh is the one-stop destination for savouring delicious Indian food. Opened by the famous Michelin-star chef, Abhi Kumar, The Dhaba offers one of the finest collection of cuisines from all around India.

The restaurant, located on the 12th floor, offers a brilliant view of the city and is accompanied by live musical performances.
The restaurant timings are as follows:
Breakfast: 7:00 to 10:00 am
Lunch: 12:00 to 3:00 pm
Dinner: 7:00 to 11:00 pm

Five-Course Menu:
