Welcome to Elita Vistas

Whether you're joining us for business or leisure, you'll be delighted with your stay at the Elita Vistas Hotel in Chandigarh. Situated in the city's business district, near Sector 17, our hotel greets you with refined luxury and a modern sense of style.

Our accommodations are designed with your comfort in mind, and are appointed with pillow top bedding, resort-style marble bathrooms and flat-screen TVs. We offer all of the facilities befitting a 5-star hotel, including an indoor/outdoor rooftop pool, a fitness centre and exceptional dining at several on-site restaurants. Visit our luxury spa for a massage while you're here as well.

If you're planning a business meeting or social event in Chandigarh, you have our flexible venue space, including an expansive lawn, at your disposal. And our convenient location places the city's attractions within your reach. Let the Elita Vistas Hotel Chandigarh exceed your expectations.
